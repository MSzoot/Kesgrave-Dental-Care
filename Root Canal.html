Root Canal Treatment

Root canal treatment is a highly effective procedure used to treat infection at the center of a tooth (the root canal system). It's designed to eliminate bacteria from the infected root canal, preventing reinfection of the tooth and saving the natural tooth.

Pain Relief: Alleviates toothache and discomfort.

Tooth Preservation: Avoids the need for tooth extraction, keeping your natural tooth.

Stops Infection: Removes bacterial infection and prevents its spread.

Restores Function: Enables the tooth to function normally again.
